About

My name is Rachit Kinger.

I blog about things in data science, digital inequality and linux that I found difficult to understand or learn. Professionally, I am head of data and analytics at a regional media company in the UK called Johnston Press. We publish close to 200 news sites that cover mainly small towns and villages in the UK. Some of our big titles include The Scotsman, The Yorkshire Post, inews (which won the British Media Awards for the website of the year - in the same year that Guardian launched its new website!).

My tech stack is simple

For data science I use R and RStudio but I am also familiar with Python for which use PyCharm with Anaconda distribution. My setup includes Dell XPS-15 (i7 quad core | 32 GB | Nvidia GPU) which I dual boot with Linux (Ubuntu 18.04) and Windows.
